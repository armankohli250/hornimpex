HornImpex – Leading Manufacturer & Exporter of Buffalo Horn and Bone Products from India

HornImpex, based in Sambhal, Uttar Pradesh – India’s renowned hub for buffalo horn and bone handicrafts – is a trusted manufacturer and exporter of premium buffalo horn and bone raw materials and finished products. Under our head brand Hornandbonecare, we specialize in eco-friendly, handcrafted, and ethically sourced buffalo horn and bone items for global buyers, wholesalers, and manufacturers.

Our Expertise

Our skilled artisans combine heritage techniques with modern precision to create products that embody elegance, durability, and natural beauty.

Our eco-conscious manufacturing process emphasizes ethical sourcing, low-waste production, and the responsible use of natural resources.

Product Range

Buffalo Horn & Bone Raw Materials: Plates, sheets, buttons, beads, blanks, toggles

Handcrafted Buffalo Horn & Bone Products: Cutlery, combs, jewelry, home décor, fashion & lifestyle accessories

Customized Bulk Orders: Tailored shapes, sizes, and designs for industrial and retail use

Why Choose HornImpex

Over two decades of export expertise in buffalo horn and bone craftsmanship

Ethically sourced materials and eco-friendly production processes

Competitive pricing for wholesale and bulk orders

Reliable global shipping and timely delivery

Trusted by international buyers for consistency and premium quality

Experience Natural Elegance

With over 20 years of experience in buffalo horn and bone manufacturing, HornImpex has earned a reputation for quality, reliability, and sustainability.

From raw buffalo horn and bone materials to finished handcrafted crafts, we ensure every piece reflects India’s artisanal legacy and meets stringent global export standards.

HornImpex supplies both industrial buyers and retailers seeking high-quality, sustainable buffalo horn and bone materials for manufacturing or resale.

Whether you need raw buffalo horn and bone materials for manufacturing or ready-to-use handcrafted products, HornImpex is your trusted global partner for premium, sustainable, and beautifully crafted horn and bone items.

Contact us today to discuss your bulk or customized export requirements. International inquiries are welcome.
